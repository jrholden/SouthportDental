About Southport Dental

Dr. Joanne graduated from Acadia University in 1985 with a BSC. Degree. She then attended Dalhousie University and graduated with her Doctor of Dental Surgery Degree in 1989.

She returned to her childhood neighborhood of Stratford to begin her dental career. July 4th, 1989 she opened the doors to her dental practice with her team member Mary Joy by her side. Twenty-nine years later they still enjoy working side by side to provide the highest quality of dental care in a friendly caring compassionate manner. She loves people and genuinely wants to make their dental experience the most positive it can be. She has a passion for dentistry that continues to grow stronger each year. She is often heard saying how much she loves the profession she chose almost 29 years ago!
